About Our School

Our history, mission, and values

Our History

Founded in 1995, Sunrise Academy began as a small community school with just 50 students. Over the years, we have grown into a leading educational institution serving over 800 students from kindergarten through 12th grade.

Our campus has expanded to include modern science labs, a library with over 20,000 books, and state-of-the-art sports facilities.

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive, challenging educational experience that prepares students for success in college, career, and life. We foster intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and ethical responsibility in a supportive, inclusive community.

Our Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in all we do
  • Integrity: Acting with honesty and moral courage
  • Respect: Valuing diversity and treating all with dignity
  • Responsibility: Being accountable for our actions and their impact
  • Community: Working together for the common good
